# Welcome to Namida

Namida is a beautiful and feature-rich music and video player with YouTube support, built in Flutter. It combines powerful local music playback with seamless YouTube integration, offering everything you might expect from a modern music player and more.

## Key Features

### Library & Indexing

- **Powerful Indexer & Tag Editor** powered by jaudiotagger
- **Artists and Genres Separators** for better organization
- **Prevent Duplicated Tracks** automatically
- **Folders-based Library System** with exclusion support
- Sort by almost any property of the track, album, artist, or genre

### Look & Feel

- **Material3-like Theme** for modern aesthetics
- **Dynamic Theming** - player colors adapt to album artwork
- **Multiple Views**: Home, Tracks, Albums, Artists, Genres, Playlists, Queues, and Folders
- **Waveform Seekbar** for visual playback navigation
- **Extensive Customization** options

### Streaming & YouTube

<Note>
Namida's YouTube integration is one of its standout features, offering functionality typically found in dedicated YouTube apps.
</Note>

- Best video & audio quality streaming
- Audio-only & data saver modes
- Download, cache & offline playback support
- **SponsorBlock** integration to skip sponsored segments
- **Return YouTube Dislike** integration
- Video view with gesture support (swipe to control volume, double tap to seek, etc.)
- Multi-library support (Local/YouTube/Subsonic/WebDAV)

### Playback Features

- **Lyrics Support** - auto-fetch synced, plain, and word-synced lyrics
- **Sleep Timer** - set by tracks or minutes
- **Crossfade** and play/pause fade effects
- **Skip Silence** functionality
- **Replay Gain** (normalize audio)
- **Equalizer** with custom presets
- Control pause scenarios (calls, notifications, volume 0, etc.)

### Smart Features

<Accordion title="Smart Track Generation">
  Generate related tracks based on listening history using an advanced algorithm. Create playlists from:
  - Tracks you often listened to in the same period
  - Tracks released around the same time
  - Specific time ranges
  - Available moods
  - Random selection
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="History & Statistics">
  - Reliable history system with customizable thresholds
  - Most played playlists based on history
  - Lost memories - rediscover tracks from years past
  - Import YouTube and Last.fm history
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Video Integration">
  Namida can play videos related to music, either:
  - From local files (automatically matched)
  - Fetched from YouTube
  - Full video library indexing (since v4.0.0)
</Accordion>
