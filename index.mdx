## Why Namida?

Namida is more than just a music player. It's a complete audio and video experience that brings together local library management, YouTube streaming, and powerful customization features in one beautiful app.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Local Library" icon="music" href="/features/library">
    Powerful indexer with tag editor, artist separators, and smart folder management
  </Card>
  <Card title="YouTube Integration" icon="youtube" href="/features/youtube">
    Stream, download, and cache YouTube content with SponsorBlock support
  </Card>
  <Card title="Video Playback" icon="video" href="/features/video-playback">
    Play local and YouTube videos with gesture controls and fullscreen support
  </Card>
  <Card title="Advanced Playlists" icon="list" href="/features/playlists">
    Create custom playlists with artwork and flexible management
  </Card>
  <Card title="Synced Lyrics" icon="closed-captioning" href="/playback/lyrics">
    Auto-fetch synced and plain lyrics with word-level synchronization
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audio Effects" icon="waveform" href="/playback/audio-effects">
    Crossfade, replay gain, pitch control, equalizer, and skip silence
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Library & Indexing" icon="database">
    - Powerful audio and video indexer with tag editor support
    - Smart artist and genre separators
    - Duplicate track prevention
    - Minimum file size and duration filters
    - Folder-based library with exclusion support
    - Sort by any track or album property
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="YouTube & Streaming" icon="youtube">
    - Best video and audio quality streaming
    - Audio-only mode and data saver options
    - Download and caching for offline playback
    - SponsorBlock integration with segment skipping
    - Return YouTube Dislike integration
    - Multi-library support: Local, YouTube, Subsonic, WebDAV
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Look & Feel" icon="palette">
    - Material3-like design language
    - Dynamic theming from album artwork
    - Waveform seekbar visualization
    - Animating thumbnail with audio peaks
    - Miniplayer party mode with edge effects
    - Extensive customization options
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Advanced Features" icon="sparkles">
    - Import YouTube and Last.fm history
    - Backup and restore system
    - Smart track generation based on listening patterns
    - History tracking with configurable thresholds
    - Most played playlists and statistics
    - Lost memories feature to rediscover old tracks
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
