## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Namida?">
    Namida is a beautiful and feature-rich music and video player with YouTube support, built in Flutter. It's available for Android (stable/beta), Windows (beta), and Linux (beta).

    Key features include:
    - Local music library management with powerful indexing
    - YouTube streaming, downloads, and offline playback
    - Video playback with gesture controls
    - Dynamic theming and Material3-like design
    - Advanced playlist management
    - History tracking and smart recommendations
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which platforms does Namida support?">
    Namida is available on:
    - **Android** (stable and beta releases)
    - **Windows** (beta)
    - **Linux** (beta)

    Download the latest version from the [GitHub releases page](https://github.com/namidaco/namida/releases).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Namida free and open source?">
    Yes, Namida is open source under a custom EULA license. You may read, compile, and modify the code for personal usage or for the purpose of contribution. However, redistributing the program under a different name or license without permission is not allowed.

    The app includes some proprietary components that remain the intellectual property of namidaco.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Namida collect any personal data?">
    No. Namida does **not** collect any personal data, usage analytics, crashlytics, or background tracking. Your privacy is fully respected and protected.

    Error logs are saved locally and can only be shared manually as separate files when you choose to do so.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features & Functionality

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can Namida play YouTube videos offline?">
    Yes! Namida supports:
    - Downloading YouTube videos and audio
    - Caching for offline playback
    - Audio-only mode and data saver mode
    - Custom download filename builder (similar to yt-dlp)
    - Cache priority system to keep important content

    You can configure download settings including quality, format, and metadata tags.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the video integration work?">
    Namida can play videos related to your music in two ways:

    **Local Videos:**
    - Looks for matching video files in your library folders
    - Matches by filename, title & artist, or YouTube ID in tags
    - Ignores symbols and whitespaces for better matching

    **YouTube Videos:**
    - Extracts YouTube links from track comment tags (often added by yt-dlp)
    - Downloads and caches videos for playback
    - Supports full video library indexing since v4.0.0
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What audio formats does Namida support?">
    Namida supports a wide range of audio formats including:
    - MP3, M4A, AAC
    - FLAC (lossless)
    - OGG, Opus
    - WAV
    - And many more

    The app uses a powerful tag editor powered by jaudiotagger for metadata management.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Namida support lyrics?">
    Yes! Namida has excellent lyrics support:
    - Auto-fetching of synced and plain lyrics
    - Support for word-synced LRC/TTML formats
    - Simple lyrics editing
    - Lyrics offset adjustment
    - Search tracks by lyrics content
    - Multi-language lyrics display
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the 'Smort Tracks Generation' feature?">
    Namida can generate smart playlists based on your listening history:
    - **Recommended Mix**: Tracks you often listened to together
    - **Similar Release Date**: Tracks released around the same time
    - **Time Range**: Tracks from specific periods
    - **Underrated Mix**: Hidden gems from your library
    - **Lost Memories**: Tracks you listened to around this time in previous years

    These features help you rediscover your music in new ways.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import my YouTube history?">
    Yes! Namida can import:
    - YouTube history (including from Google Takeout)
    - Last.fm scrobbling history
    - Multiple history files at once
    - YouTube playlists from CSV files

    The app will merge your imported history with local listening data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Customization & Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What customization options are available?">
    Namida offers extensive customization:
    - **Dynamic theming** from album artwork
    - **Waveform seekbar** with animation
    - **Miniplayer party mode** with edge breathing effects
    - **Particles effect** that responds to audio peaks
    - **Animating thumbnail** synced to audio
    - Custom color palettes
    - Grid layouts and view modes
    - Configurable swipe actions
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the sleep timer work?">
    Namida includes a sleep timer with two modes:
    - **Timer by minutes**: Stop playback after a set duration
    - **Timer by tracks**: Stop after a specific number of tracks

    You can configure it from the player controls.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What audio effects are available?">
    Namida supports various audio enhancements:
    - **Crossfade** between tracks
    - **Play/Pause fade** effects
    - **Skip silence** functionality
    - **Replay Gain** (normalize volume)
    - **Equalizer** with presets
    - **Pitch and speed** control
    - **Volume normalization**
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## YouTube Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What YouTube features does Namida support?">
    Namida includes comprehensive YouTube integration:
    - Best video and audio quality streaming
    - SponsorBlock integration
    - Return YouTube Dislike integration
    - Video segments and heatmaps
    - Channel subscriptions (when signed in)
    - Playlists management
    - Comments (view, add, edit, delete)
    - Like/dislike videos
    - Data saver mode
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Namida without a YouTube account?">
    Yes! Most YouTube features work without signing in. However, some features like:
    - Channel subscriptions
    - Personalized recommendations
    - Playlist creation and management
    - Liking/commenting on videos

    These require you to sign in with your YouTube account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the cache system work?">
    Namida uses a smart cache priority system:
    - **VIP priority**: Unavailable videos, important content
    - **High priority**: Frequently accessed media
    - **Normal priority**: Regular cached content
    - **Low priority**: Can be cleared automatically

    You can manually set cache priority for specific videos to prevent them from being cleared.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Library Management

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I prevent duplicate tracks?">
    Namida includes built-in duplicate prevention:
    - Set minimum file size and duration in settings
    - Automatic duplicate detection during indexing
    - Option to prevent duplicated artist tracks
    - Smart matching algorithm

    Configure these options in **Settings > Indexer**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I organize music by folders?">
    Yes! Namida supports folder-based organization:
    - Add specific folders to your library
    - Exclude folders you don't want indexed
    - Dedicated folders page with navigation
    - Support for `cover.jpg` as folder artwork
    - Support for `.info.txt` for folder descriptions
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I edit track tags?">
    Namida includes a powerful tag editor:
    1. Long press on any track
    2. Select **Edit Tags** from the menu
    3. Modify title, artist, album, year, genre, and more
    4. You can edit multiple tracks at once

    <Note>
      Tag editing requires **All Files Access** permission on Android 10+.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where can I get help if I have issues?">
    If you encounter problems:
    1. Check the [Troubleshooting guide](/community/troubleshooting)
    2. Share logs from **Settings > About > Share Logs**
    3. Report issues on [GitHub](https://github.com/namidaco/namida/issues)
    4. Ask in the community channels (Telegram, Discord)

    Always include logs when reporting bugs for faster resolution.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I report a bug?">
    To report a bug:
    1. Go to **Settings > About > Share Logs**
    2. Create an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/namidaco/namida/issues)
    3. Use the **[BUG]** template
    4. Include steps to reproduce
    5. Attach the log files
    6. Add screenshots if possible
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
